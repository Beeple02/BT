HOW DARK POOL TRADING WORKS
WHAT IS A DARK POOL?
A dark pool is a private trading venue where large orders are matched without being visible to the public lit market. This protects institutional traders from adverse price movement caused by their own order flow — known as market impact.
EXECUTION MECHANICS
Midpoint Pricing — All dark pool trades execute at the exact midpoint of the National Best Bid and Offer (NBBO). This ensures fair pricing for both sides with zero spread cost.
Anti-Gaming Protection — The platform uses time-weighted average midpoint pricing to prevent predatory strategies from gaming the midpoint reference.
15-Minute Delayed Reporting — Completed block trades are reported to the tape with a 15-minute delay, minimizing market impact from other participants front-running your execution.
IOI Board — Indications of Interest are displayed with no identifying information — only direction, size tier, and urgency. This allows institutions to signal interest and find natural counterparties.
